Our Services

Professional geological and environmental consulting services delivered by Rudra Pratap Singh

Mining Plan

Comprehensive Mining Plans for both Major and Minor Minerals, ensuring regulatory compliance and sustainable extraction methodologies.

  • Major Mineral Mining Plans
  • Minor Mineral Approvals
  • Renewal & Modification

Environment Clearance

Complete Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) and Environmental Management Plan (EMP) for hassle-free clearances from MoEFCC.

  • EIA/EMP Reports
  • Public Hearings
  • Compliance Monitoring

CTO & CTE

Consent to Operate (CTO) and Consent to Establish (CTE) from Uttar Pradesh Pollution Control Board (UPPCB) and other SPCBs.

  • Air & Water Consent
  • Hazardous Waste Auth
  • Annual Compliance

Groundwater Permission

CGWA (Central Ground Water Authority) and UPGWD (Uttar Pradesh Ground Water Department) permissions and NOCs.

  • CGWA NOC
  • UPGWD Registration
  • Rainwater Harvesting

Geological Exploration

Comprehensive exploration services for precious and base metals with advanced survey techniques and resource estimation.

  • Geological Mapping
  • Drilling Supervision
  • Resource Estimation

Infrastructure Geo-Consulting

Geological consultancy for major infrastructure projects including railways, waterways, and construction.

  • Geotechnical Investigation
  • Foundation Analysis
  • Material Testing
